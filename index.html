Can you describe your process in more detail?

We have a simple 4-step process:

  1. Gather Info
  2. Design
  3. Develop
  4. Test and Delivery

Phase One: Gather Info

We’ll need a few minutes of your time to understand your vision for the site, what your goals are for visitors and the content you want included.

Have an existing site or other marketing materials? Great! We’ll be able to leverage this content and the process will go a lot faster.

Phase Two: Design

Now it’s our turn. We’ll get together and create a plan for your site, including a sitemap, appropriate calls-to-action and a fitting visual design.

Next, we’ll share the design with you and make sure it meets your expectations. As always, we expect to make a few tweaks and changes at this point so that you are as excited with the final vision as we are.

Phase Three: Develop

Once we’re 100% on the same page, it’s time to get into the nitty-gritty. We have to incorporate your branding elements, our graphic elements and code them into the home page and all other content pages.

This is the meaty part of the work, when all site functionality is developed.

Phase Four: Test and Deliver

A lot of web developers hate testing. Not us! We know that an untested website is a broken site. We check the code, test in all browsers and with mobile devices. We have test users try the site out, uncoached, to see if they can use it intuitively and easily.

At this point we’ll ask you to do some testing on your end to make sure we met your expectations. Once you accept the final product, we’ll migrate the site to your final domain and release it to the wild!
